Mission


The New York City Asian American Student Conference (NYCAASC) is an intercollegiate organization dedicated to empowering and engaging individuals in addressing Asian/Pacific/Americans issues on local, national, and global scales.

Organized by representatives across high schools and universities in the New York City metropolitan area, NYCAASC provides a necessary, accessible space for student leaders and activists to develop knowledge and engage in racial and sociopolitical dialogue since 2007. Through interactive workshops, performers and local and national speakers, the conference is structured to encourage solidarity and initiative within the New York City community and beyond.
