Nancy Ng


Nancy is a third year PhD student studying clinical psychology at the New School for Social Research. Her research interests center on the roles of race, ethnicity, and culture in therapeutic processes involving Asian Americans. As an Asian American growing up in Hong Kong and immigrating to the U.S. at a young age, Nancy understands the struggle families go through during the acculturation process. Her externship is at NYU Lutheran Medical Center where provides mental health services to the Asian American community.

The New York City Asian American Student Conference 2016.
