Track 2: Around

$10 Dessert or $5 Noodles? Our Role in the Gentrification of Chinatown and NYC


Description

We don’t think much of spending $10 on artisan ice cream with egg waffles and toppings galore. In fact, these types of businesses are bringing in the big bucks. Yet the local noodle shop on the corner, making $5 bowls of wontons and noodles, is struggling. Did you ever think about why it has become more expensive for certain people to sell and even live in Chinatown? Perhaps our spending choices are changing the demographics and costs of living in New York City, and in effect displacing those who are less privileged. We will explore the role of today's youth on gentrification, including in that of Manhattan's Chinatown, and explore commonalities between New York City’s gentrifying neighborhoods.
