Track 2: Around

Women's Liberation: From New York City to the Philippines


Description

This workshop focuses on women's oppression and resistance throughout the Pilipinx diaspora. This workshop will examine what conditions create such oppressions and how even in sites of oppression, women's resistance is real and possible. We will examine the connections among oppression, migration, diaspora, and patriarchy as well as discuss the significance of global news cases such as the murder of Jennifer Laude, a trans Pinay sex worker, the case of Mary Jane Veloso, a migrant worker framed for trafficking drugs now sitting on death row, and the naming of Pia Wurtzbach (Ms Philippines 2016) as Ms Universe 2016.
