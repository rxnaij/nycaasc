Track 3: Beyond

Educators for Black Lives: Perspectives of Asian American educators on teaching our youth of color


Description

A classroom “simulation” and conversation with Teach For America alumni educators, led by Teach For America’s Asian American & Pacific Islander Initiative, will give voice to how they’re thinking about the Black Lives Matter (BLM) movement in the context of education and working with youth. As the BLM movement continues, we must listen to one another to inform our conversations, coalitions, and actions. This simulation and conversation will allow for opportunity to share around how we all engage in work across lines of difference and power, and will incorporate elements of our past to inform the conversation.
