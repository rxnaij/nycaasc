Track 1: Within

The Internalities of Race: Is There Something More to Being Asian?


Description

This workshop explores the non-national affiliations within the A/P/A community, ones that are often overlooked when speaking of identity. We will address concerns over the unspoken obligation to assimilate into a larger group at the expense of other parts of our backgrounds. A panel of speakers will speak on how identification and de-identification shapes the boundaries in the community. Shashank Rao will be moderating the discussion, helping it along with guiding questions and participating in the discussion.
