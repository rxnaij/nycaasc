Track 2: Noncompliant Bodies


Kalaya'an Mendoza


Kalaya'an Mendoza currently serves as Amnesty International USA's Field Director. In his role he has coordinated Human Rights Delegations to Ferguson, trained activists across the country around nonviolent direct action and integrated anti-oppression and decolonization work into human rights activism. He's a loud and proud Queer Pin@y from the Left Coast and is an accomplished Earthbender.
