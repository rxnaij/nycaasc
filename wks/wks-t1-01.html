Track 1: Within

Trash Talk: Disposability and its Role in Sustainability

Description

In this workshop, we'll be getting up close and personal with something everyone comes in contact with on a daily basis: trash! What is trash? Where does it come from? Where does it go? We will be re-imagining the concept of trash and its relationship to sustainability and environmentalism in a historical, political, and racial context.
