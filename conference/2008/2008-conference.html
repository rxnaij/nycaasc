Keynote Speaker

Dennis Chin, Keynote Speaker

Dennis Chin, 23, is everything your ideal community organizer should be--sharp, progressive, articulate, committed, humorous...and well-dressed. A 2006 alum of The College of New Jersey, Dennis has been involved in organizations, campaigns, and movements to advance social justice and equality for years now. As an Advocacy Co-Chair of the 2005-2007 National Asian American Student Conference board, he helped to initiate NAASCon's nationwide Asian American Studies campaign, and helped to promote their second biennial conference at Northwestern University in November 2006. He also delivered the keynote speech for the Southeast Regional Conference of Asian American Leaders this past fall at the University of Florida. Currently, Dennis works as a Program Associate for the Center for Community Change in New York City. He has helped to launch CCC's Movement Vision Lab, a groundbreaking on-line website for grassroots organizers and social justice activists to find, share, rate, and debate visionary ideas for the future.
