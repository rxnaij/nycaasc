Sponsorship Opportunities

Every year, our students collaboratively provide resources for attendees to engage with New York City’s Asian/Pacific/American community. By sponsoring us, you help us support our mission and ensure the continuation of this essential space.

By the Numbers

Expand Your Reach

As our Community Partner, your organization has the opportunity to gain wider recognition from a student population across multiple campuses through our various social media and on-campus outlets.

Previous sponsors include cultural organizations, such as Kollaboration, Anakbayan, CAAAV, as well as international companies, such as McDonald’s, Warner Bros, and Starbucks.

Tiers

All donations to NYCAASC are fully tax-deductible.

Bronze (contributions of $25 or more)

• thank you announcement on social media

• listed as sponsor on program distributed to attendees

Silver (contributions of $50 or more)

• all bronze benefits

• logo & website link on NYCAASC website with ad space, subject to approval

• recognition on conference promotional materials

Gold (contributions of $100 or more)

• all bronze and silver benefits

• logo and mention in NYCAASC 2016 closing email

• NYCAASC representatives to attend your organization’s events and student networking within Spring 2016

Platinum (contributions of $150 or more)

• all bronze, silver, and gold benefits

• special thanks during opening and closing ceremony

All-Star (contributions of $500 or more)

• all bronze, silver, gold, and platinum benefits

• custom sponsor graphic or advertisement up to 4.25 x 5.5in to appear on the NYCAASC program
